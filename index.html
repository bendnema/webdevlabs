Digital Student ID

Transforming campus accessibility with a modern, secure, and convenient mobile solution for student identification.

Learn More

The Problem

In an increasingly digital world, physical student ID cards are becoming an outdated and inefficient system on college campuses.

Challenges with Physical ID Cards:

  • Easy to misplace, forget, or damage
  • Creates inconvenience and safety concerns
  • Inefficient replacement processes
  • Despite 97% of students owning smartphones, most colleges still rely on plastic cards
  • Limited adoption of digital solutions due to integration challenges or platform restrictions
  • Costly systems like CBoard that are not always efficient or effective

For universities, physical cards mean higher administrative costs for replacement and lost access incidents, while also presenting avoidable security risks.

Our Solution

A cross-platform digital student ID app that allows students to carry their identification directly on their smartphones.

Business Plan

Our innovative Digital Student ID solution for modern campus experiences

Our business model is based on long-term service contracts with higher education institutions, offering a cross-platform digital student ID app that replaces outdated physical cards with smartphone technology.

Our revenue structure includes:

  • Annual licensing fees based on student population size
  • Additional revenue from integration services
  • Optional premium features like analytics dashboards and attendance tracking

Our pricing tiers provide flexibility for institutions of all sizes:

  • Free launch program for ~50 students to encourage adoption
  • 250 students: $750 annually
  • 500 students: $1,125 annually
  • 1,000 students: $2,000 annually
  • 2,500 students: $4,500 annually
  • 5,000 students: $7,500 annually
  • Custom quotes for larger institutions

We're currently seeking partnerships with forward-thinking universities for pilot programs and investors who share our vision of modernizing campus technology infrastructure.
